Achievements

Who we are?. It’ll blow your mind.

Hacked annually hosted highly anticipated hackathons, drawing in hundreds of participants from various disciplines and skill levels. These events weren't just about coding; they were about fostering collaboration, creativity, and problem-solving. Over intense 48-hour sessions, teams brainstormed, coded, and polished their ideas, resulting in a plethora of innovative projects that tackled real-world issues. From developing apps to optimize public transportation routes to creating AI-driven solutions for mental health support, the outcomes of these hackathons were as diverse as the participants themselves. Some projects even evolved beyond the event, becoming viable startups or open-source initiatives, making tangible impacts on communities worldwide.

Oh yeah, it’s that good. See for yourself.

The software development prowess of Hacked members was demonstrated through a multitude of projects that pushed the boundaries of technology. From crafting intuitive mobile applications for tracking endangered species to designing sophisticated web platforms for connecting volunteers with disaster relief efforts, our club consistently delivered high-quality, user-centric solutions.

And lastly, this one. Checkmate.

Strategic partnerships were instrumental in Hacked's growth and success. By collaborating with leading tech companies such as Google, Microsoft, and Amazon Web Services, we gained access to invaluable resources, including cloud credits, software licenses, and mentorship programs.
