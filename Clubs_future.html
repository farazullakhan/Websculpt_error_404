Our Future

In the future, our college club "Hacked" envisions expanding its influence and reach through various avenues. One potential direction involves the establishment of satellite chapters or branches on other campuses, broadening our impact across different universities. Additionally, as our club gains recognition and credibility within the cybersecurity community, we aspire to forge partnerships with industry leaders. These collaborations could lead to opportunities for hosting events at corporate headquarters, participating in industry conferences, or even securing sponsorship for our activities. Furthermore, with the growth of our membership and activities, we aim to secure dedicated space on campus, providing a hub for cybersecurity enthusiasts to meet, collaborate, and innovate.
